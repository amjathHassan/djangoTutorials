Kesavan Venugopal, better known by his stage name Nedumudi Venu, was an Indian actor and screenwriter from Kerala, who predominantly worked in Malayalam cinema. He acted in more than 500 films, primarily in Malayalam and also in Tamil in a career spanning nearly five decades. Wikipedia

Born: 22 May 1948, Alappuzha

Died: 11 October 2021, KIMSHEALTH Hospital Trivandrum, Thiruvananthapuram

Spouse: T. R. Susheela

Children: Unni Gopal, Kannan Gopal

Parents: P. K. Kesavan Nair, P. Kunjikkuttiyamma
